affinity:

interactive media

With the class of Product Designer, they tend to have a strong affinity towards interactive media. The choices presented in interactive media art forms like video games, AR/VR experiences, and installations offer a lot to reference for personal projects. There has been a strong connection to interactive media since childhood. Finding new ways to play and participate in social environments through media has always been a passion for them. As technology advances, they can only imagine the ways they could play.

*hardcore fan of Nintendo games and fantasy rpgs. I love to game when I can, as a treat.
